About Election Commission of India

Constitutional Authority ensuring free and fair elections

Overview

The Election Commission of India (ECI) is an autonomous constitutional authority responsible for administering election processes in India.

Constitutional Status

Established under Article 324 of the Indian Constitution, the ECI ensures democratic elections are conducted fairly.

Responsibilities

Conducting elections, maintaining voter rolls, enforcing election laws, and ensuring transparency.
