Election Process

Step-by-step journey of voting in India

1. Voter Registration

Citizens register themselves in the electoral roll.

2. Voter ID Verification

Eligibility is verified by Election Commission.

3. Casting Vote

Voters cast their vote at assigned polling booths.

4. Counting of Votes

Votes are securely counted after polling ends.

5. Declaration of Results

Final results are announced transparently.
