Voter Awareness

Know your rights and responsibilities as a voter

Right to Vote

Every eligible citizen has the right to vote freely in elections.

Voter Eligibility

Citizens aged 18 and above can register as voters.

Ethical Voting

Vote responsibly without any influence or pressure.

Election Guidelines

Follow rules and maintain discipline at polling stations.
